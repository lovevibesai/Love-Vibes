## 7. Risk Mitigation & Cost Ceilings

### **Recursive Query Risk (D1)**
**Problem:** Unoptimized queries can explode read costs
**Solution:** S2 cell indexing already implemented, prevents 1M+ row scans
**Monitoring:** Query performance tracked in Analytics Engine

### **Zombie Chat Duration (Durable Objects)**
**Problem:** Idle WebSocket connections charge for duration
**Solution:** 60-second inactivity timeout implemented
**Cost ceiling:** $600/month at 100K concurrent chats (1M DAU scenario)

### **Media Egress Threshold**
**Problem:** High video traffic may trigger enterprise tier requirement
**Solution:** 
- Video compression (H.265, 1MB average)
- CDN caching (Cloudflare automatically caches R2)
- Lazy loading (fetch on-demand, not preload)
**Estimated trigger:** >500K DAU with heavy video usage

### **D1 Write Limits**
**Problem:** Paid plan includes 50M writes/month
**Solution:** 
- Batch profile updates
- Use KV for ephemeral data (online status, typing indicators)
- Write coalescing (combine multiple updates)
**Overage cost:** $1 per 1M additional writes
