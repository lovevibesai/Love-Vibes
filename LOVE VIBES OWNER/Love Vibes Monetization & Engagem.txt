## 11. Compliance & Ethics

### 11.1 Legal Requirements

**Subscription Disclosures:**
```
- Clear auto-renewal terms before purchase
- Easy cancellation (within app, no contact required)
- Prorated refunds for early cancellation (first 30 days)
- Comply with App Store/Google Play policies
```

**Data Privacy:**
```
- Payment data never stored (handled by Stripe/Apple/Google)
- Spending history visible in "Billing History"
- Delete account = delete all transaction history (GDPR)
```

**Age Restrictions:**
```
- 18+ only (verified at signup)
- No targeting minors in marketing
- Safe Harbor compliance for payment processing
```

### 11.2 Ethical Guardrails

**No Dark Patterns:**
```
âŒ NEVER: Fake urgency ("Only 2 credits left at this price!")
âŒ NEVER: Hidden fees (all prices upfront)
âŒ NEVER: Difficult cancellation (always 2 taps max)
âŒ NEVER: Bait-and-switch (promised features always delivered)
```

**Transparent Pricing:**
```
âœ… ALWAYS: Show cost before action
âœ… ALWAYS: Confirm purchases with second tap
âœ… ALWAYS: Clearly label "free" vs "paid" features
âœ… ALWAYS: Honor advertised prices (no dynamic pricing discrimination)
```

**Accessibility:**
```
âœ… Core features (match, chat) free forever
âœ… No pay-to-win mechanics (paid users don't suppress free users)
âœ… Alternative paths (earn credits vs buy credits)
âœ… Financial hardship: Disable account option (pause, not delete)
```
