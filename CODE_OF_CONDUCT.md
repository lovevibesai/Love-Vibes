# Love Vibes Team Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
As members of the Love Vibes development team, we pledge to foster a professional, collaborative, and inclusive environment. We are committed to technical excellence and mutual respect.

## Our Standards
- **Excellence**: We strive for high-quality, maintainable, and secure code.
- **Collaboration**: We help each other grow and share knowledge openly within the team.
- **Respect**: We treat all team members with professional courtesy.
- **Integrity**: We take responsibility for our work and communicate openly about challenges.

## Confidentiality
As this is a proprietary project, all team members are bound by confidentiality agreements. Discussion of internal architecture, proprietary algorithms, or sensitive data outside of authorized channels is strictly prohibited.
